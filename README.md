E-commerce's introduction has changed the face of traditional retail, emphasizing the importance of comprehending and analyzing customer behavior. The objective of this project is to use Microsoft Power BI's analytical skills to mine the abundance of data onAmazon in order to identify intricate patterns and trends in customer preferences. For companies looking for evidence-based solutions in the ever-changing
realm of online retail, the study acts as a road map.
Amazon's extensive dataset contains a wide range of data, such as product specifications and consumer interactions. This abundance of data creates an ideal environment for in-depth analysis, enabling us to derive detailed and thorough insights about customer behaviour. The impact of Amazon on international e-commerce is unmatched. Knowing how customers behave on this
platform offers a window into future trends in addition to a snapshot of existing ones. Amazon's ecosystem can provide insights that can guide initiatives that are applicable to the larger e-commerce market. Consumer behaviour analytics directly translate into strategic advantages for companies who operate on Amazon. The ramifications of having a sophisticated understanding of consumer behaviour are extensive and range from improving advertising tactics to fine-tuning product offerings. Being able to modify and personalize plans in
response to data insights is essential for success in the cutthroat world of e-commerce.
